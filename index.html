How the Process Works

At EGN Web Design, I make the entire website process simple, streamlined, and completely worry‑free. I handle all the technical work — design, setup, structure, optimization, and updates — so you can stay focused on running your business. My goal is to remove the stress and confusion that usually comes with building a website.
